Powerful Features

📜

Board Evolution

Explore the history of EMWaver hardware. A visual archive of all 20+ board versions and their design stories.

📡

Sub-GHz Radio

Control and analyze sub-GHz signals with CC1101 transceiver. Perfect for IoT, remote controls, and wireless experimentation.

🔴

Infrared Control

Send and receive IR signals. Learn, analyze, and control IR devices with built-in IR transmitter and receiver.

⚡

Wavelets

Extend functionality with JavaScript-based Wavelets. Create custom UIs and device workflows without firmware changes.

🔌

16 GPIO Pins

Connect any hardware you need. Full GPIO access for sensors, displays, and custom peripherals.

📱

Mobile Apps

Native iOS and Android apps for device control, signal analysis, and Wavelet management.

💻

CLI Tool

Command-line interface for automation, scripting, and headless device control.
