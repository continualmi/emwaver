In this video we do a fully plug-and-play walkthrough of EMWaver’s Infrared + Sub‑GHz (ISM) tooling across Android, iOS, and Desktop—recording, saving, analyzing, and reliably retransmitting real signals with no firmware coding or flashing.

Devices used
- Infrared Waver (IR RX/TX over USB)
- ISM Waver (CC1101 Sub‑GHz over USB)
- EMWaver flagship (IR + CC1101, BLE to iPhone)

What you’ll see
- Record an IR remote and inspect the captured pulses in the Sampler view
- Save captures and move the same signals across Android ↔ iOS ↔ Desktop
- Use the CC1101 Wavelet to init RX/TX at 433 MHz and verify configuration (frequency/modulation)
- Record a garage remote (Sub‑GHz), then retransmit and re-capture to confirm a 1:1 match
- Cross-device + cross-platform parity: same UI, same workflow, same results

Notes
- iOS uses BLE (ESP32‑S3 devices like the flagship). STM32 USB devices (Infrared/ISM Waver) are used on Desktop/Android.
- Only test/clone/transmit against devices and systems you own or have explicit permission to work with.
