Emwaver v1.0: The Breadboard Proto

The very first proof-of-concept. This version proved that the ESP32-S3 could directly drive the CC1101 without a dedicated coprocessor. However, the antenna placement was suboptimal, causing significant interference with the USB data lines.

Emwaver v2.0: First SMD Attempt

This was our first attempt at a surface-mount design. We moved to a more compact form factor, but still struggled with RF noise, especially near the USB lines.

Emwaver v5.0: The USB-C Leap

A major redesign saw the introduction of USB-C, solving the physical durability issues. We moved to a 4-layer PCB stackup to better isolate the RF signals.
