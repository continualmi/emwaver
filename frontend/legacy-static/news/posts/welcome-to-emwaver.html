Welcome to EMWaver

January 9, 2025

Welcome to the EMWaver blog! This is where we'll share updates, news, and insights about EMWaver hardware and software development.

What is EMWaver?

EMWaver is an all-in-one platform for wireless experimentation, device control, and embedded development. It combines:

  • ESP32-S3 microcontroller
  • CC1101 sub-GHz radio transceiver
  • Infrared transmitter and receiver
  • 16 GPIO pins for custom hardware
  • Mobile apps for iOS and Android
  • CLI tool for automation
